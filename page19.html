Materuni Waterfall

Materuni Waterfall

Would you like

An excursion to a waterfall is an excellent opportunity to relax in solitude surrounded only by
nature. It is also excellent preparation for the climb atop Africa's highest peak.

On the way

The path to the waterfall is replete with sights of growing coffee, mangoes, avocados, lemons and bananas. Upon reaching the waterfall,you will come upon Materuni's refreshing currents.

At the base

Not only will you feel refreshed but you will revel in the beauty of the African wilderness. The waterfall is so
enchanting that you may well be entranced for hours by this marvelous sight.
